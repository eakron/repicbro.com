Cheatsheet

Change subreddit
Just change the end of the url so that it says /r/yourfavouritesubreddit, or enter your subreddit on the start page
A personal favourite is repicbro.com/r/minecraft!

Toggle NSFW
Click the 'NSFW' link in the navigation bar, or press 'n'.

Next and previous picture
Move between pictures with left and right arrow button, or 'j' and 'k'

View original post
Just click on the title
